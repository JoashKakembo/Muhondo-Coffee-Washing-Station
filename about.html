About Us

Muhondo Coffee Washing Station

Where quality meets sustainability and community empowerment. Located in Muhondo Sector, Gakenke District, in Rwanda’s Northern Province, we are a private coffee processing station committed to producing some of the finest specialty coffee in the world.

Established in 2006 by the Terimbere Kawa Yacu Cooperative and fully operated by Muhondo Coffee Company Ltd. since 2013, we have grown to become a beacon of excellence in Rwanda’s coffee industry. Our dedication to quality has earned us global recognition, including the 2014 Cup of Excellence Presidential Award and the 3rd place in 2015 Cup of Excellence, solidifying our place among the best coffee producers.

What We Do

At Muhondo Coffee Washing Station

We process coffee cherries sourced from over 1,800 farmers, 40% of whom are women. Alongside our own plantation of 30,000 coffee trees, we work tirelessly to transform freshly harvested cherries into export-ready green beans. Our comprehensive process includes:

  • Agricultural training and access to essential farming inputs.
  • Financial assistance, including school fees, health insurance, and food provisions.
  • Community projects like clean water supply and farmer loans.
  • Job creation, especially for women and youth.
    • We partner with other coffee washing stations, such as Bukonya Coffee and Cyondo Coffee, to expand our capacity and impact.

Why Choose Us

Our Vision

To be a global leader in specialty coffee production while empowering farmers and fostering sustainable development in the communities we serve.

Why Choose Us

Our coffee is grown under ideal conditions, with altitudes ranging from 1,700 to 2,200 meters and a focus on the premium Arabica Bourbon variety. Our commitment to sustainability includes environmental protection measures, natural drying methods, and partnerships with organizations like the Starbucks Farmers Support Center-Rwanda for quality enhancement.

Global Reach
